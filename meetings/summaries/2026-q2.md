# OpenRail Technical Committee Summary Report 2026 Q2

The second quarter of 2026 focused on **community engagement, project governance, and event preparations** for the OpenRail Technical Committee.

The **Hack4Rail** initiative saw the closure of challenge submissions and the opening of participant registration, with OpenRail selecting a jury representative to oversee the process. 
Significant progress was made in project governance, particularly the validation of the new **Netzgrafik-Editor** governance document, permitting smoother collaboration with SNCF and future contributors. 

The TC received the application of **MscViewer** project. Already used in production by SBB. The application is waiting for a board vote.

The committee also discussed AI generated contributions. We looked at legal, quality, and reputation.

Preparations for **InnoTrans 2026** advanced, with a stand, demos, and presentations scheduled for September 22–24.

On the infrastructure front, some tooling was published allowing document geneation like the **Q2 Metrics Report**.

As the TC moves into the second half of 2026, it remains focus on transparency, refining governance structures, and ensuring the success of upcoming events.
