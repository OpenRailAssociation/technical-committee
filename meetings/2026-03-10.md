### NPM Organization for OpenRail Association (ORA)

- The OpenRail Association already has a **PyPI organization**: [OpenRail on PyPI](https://pypi.org/org/openrail/).
- A new **NPM organization** can be created, though the name `@openrail` is **not available** ([check availability](https://www.npmjs.com/search?q=%40openrail)).
- Existing packages can be **deprecated** and migrated to the new organization.
- **Note:** Crates.io does not support an organization system, so this option is not applicable there.
